The Best Movies & Series According to Ved

My top 3 movies/series of all-time.

Breaking Bad

If you're looking for a show with a compelling story, complex characters, high stakes, and exceptional production quality, Breaking Bad is definitely worth watching. It's a show that will stay with you long after the credits roll.

Puss in Boots:Last wish

"The Last Wish" is designed to be enjoyable as a standalone film. So, if you're looking for an animated movie with stunning visuals, exciting action, humor, and a surprisingly heartfelt story, "Puss in Boots: The Last Wish" is a great choice for you.

Air

As a basketball fan "Air" tells the true story of how Michael Jordan and Nike came together to create the iconic Air Jordan brand. It's a fascinating look behind the scenes of a cultural phenomenon.The film captures the excitement and energy of basketball, particularly in the 1980s when Michael Jordan was rising to stardom.
